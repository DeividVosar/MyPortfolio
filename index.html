About Me

Hello! My name is Deivid Võsar, and I’m an aspiring developer with a strong background in automation and mechatronics. My career began in hands-on engineering, from production and electrical work to complex automation systems, giving me a deep understanding of how technology integrates with real-world processes.

Over time, that foundation evolved into a passion for software development, where I now focus on building efficient, logical, and well-structured solutions. I’ve completed a Programming microdegree at TalTech, learning Java and Python, and have continued expanding my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Lua through both personal projects and professional automation work.

This transition from machines to code wasn’t accidental, it’s a natural evolution of my problem-solving mindset. My background allows me to think in systems, connect hardware and software perspectives, and approach programming challenges with a grounded, analytical view.

Today, I’m driven by the same curiosity that guided me in engineering: building things that work beautifully and efficiently. My goal is to keep advancing as a developer, blending my technical knowledge with modern software practices to deliver smart, reliable solutions in the IT world.
